These were the best days of this year

Every once in a while we all need to take a break away from the rigmaroles of our every day lives and routine; to relax, unwind and immerse ourselves in blissful peace, minus any agendas. A forest vacation is the perfect escape to the pristine wilderness, that promises uninterrupted moments of natural calm and unhindered, enchanting views to savour every moment.


Taking a vacation into nature is an exceptionally immersive experience, much divergent and unique than any other vacation that we would have taken.


Forests offer a peaceful haven, far from the maddening hustle bustle of city lives and city lights. An ideal destination to experience a flourishing natural landscape, abundant in flora and fauna and be one with the quiet and peace that it has to offer. It is an ideal place to disconnect from our screens and technology and be one with our origins, a place where we actually originated from.


Forests are known to contribute to our good health with the abundance of fresh air, natural surroundings that help the stress levels go down marginally, and at the same time oxygenating our lungs manifold. Forests are natural healers that play a vital role in refurbishing our physical and emotional well being.


Unlike zoos, where the wild life is found in enclosures and cages, far removed from their natural habitat, forests offer an incredible opportunity of closely observing and watching wild life in all its realistic glory, right in the habitat that they belong to. This unique experience is a complete feast to the senses.


Forests with their all encompassing serenity and mysterious stories, are part of our natural heritage and an ideal getaway where families can come together in love, joy and camaraderies. Forests indeed are an ideal place to bond with our loved ones, under the umbrella of our ancestral origins.
