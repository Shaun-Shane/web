Our History

Our company was founded in 1997, since 2003, Jiahua travel package not only special plane, but also special train and cruise. At the end of 2010, the first Taiwan charter plane was opened in China, creating a miracle of attendance rate of 99.4%, satisfaction rate of 99.7% and zero complaint. In July 2012, direct charter flights from Jinan to Bangkok were opened.

In 2016, it won the "top 20 Shandong tourism" together with famous enterprises such as Taishan and Qufu "three holes". It is one of the largest tourism groups with the most complete network, the highest level, the largest scale and the most complete functions in Shandong Province.
